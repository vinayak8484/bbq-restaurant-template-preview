The Concept

Smoke is an
ingredient

Ember began with one question: what happens when you apply fine dining technique to American barbecue? The answer is a restaurant that respects the pit and the plate equally.

🔥
Live Fire Kitchen
Open hearth visible from every seat. Guests watch every cut finish over the fire.
🪵
Single-Origin Wood
White oak from Pennsylvania. Apple from the Hudson Valley. Sourced by season.
🥩
Heritage Breeds
Waygu, Berkshire pork, pasture-raised poultry. Every protein named and sourced.
🍷
Smoke & Wine
A 200-bottle list curated specifically around smoked and charred flavour profiles.
